Omnichannel & Fulfillment
Buy Online, Pick Up In-Store (BOPIS): Since you already have branches, let users purchase an item online and schedule a pickup at their nearest location to save on shipping.

Dynamic Delivery Fee Calculator: Automatically calculate shipping costs at checkout based on the distance between the user's location and the nearest dispatching branch.

Restock Notifications: If an item is out of stock (either globally or at a user's preferred branch), add a "Notify Me" button so they get an email or SMS when it returns.

Subscription or Recurring Orders: If you sell consumable goods, let users set up automatic reordering (e.g., delivering the same item every 30 days).

User Experience (UX) & Product Discovery
AI-Powered Product Recommendations: Add sections like "Frequently Bought Together" or "Customers Who Viewed This Also Bought" to increase average order value.

Advanced Filtering and Sorting: Let customers narrow down searches by specific attributes like price range, brand, color, size, and average customer rating.

Product Comparison Tool: Allow users to select 2–4 products and view their specifications, prices, and features side-by-side in a table.

Wishlist and "Save for Later": Give users a way to bookmark products they are interested in but aren't quite ready to purchase yet.

Recently Viewed Items Carousel: Add a section at the bottom of the page showing the last few products the user clicked on, making navigation seamless.

Augmented Reality (AR) or 360° Views: Allow customers to spin the product in 3D or use their phone camera to see how an item (like furniture or electronics) looks in their room.

Dark Mode / Light Mode Toggle: Give users the ability to switch the website's theme for a more comfortable browsing experience at night.

Checkout & Payments
Guest Checkout: Reduce cart abandonment by allowing users to buy products by just entering an email and shipping address, without forcing them to create an account.

Multiple Payment Gateways: Integrate a variety of options including credit/debit cards, local mobile banking wallets, and "Buy Now, Pay Later" (BNPL) services.

Abandoned Cart Recovery: Set up automated emails or SMS messages that remind users if they leave the website with items still sitting in their shopping cart.

One-Click Checkout: For returning logged-in users, allow them to securely save their payment and address details to buy items instantly.

Customer Retention & Community
User-Generated Content (UGC) in Reviews: Expand your planned review system to allow customers to upload their own photos or videos of the product they received.

Loyalty and Rewards Program: Award customers points for purchases, leaving reviews, or referring friends, which they can redeem for discounts on future orders.

Comprehensive User Dashboard: Create a profile area where users can track active orders, view past purchase history, manage saved addresses, and check reward points.

Live Chat or AI Chatbot: Add a support widget in the corner of the screen to help users instantly with basic queries like "What are your shipping times?" or "Where is my order?"

Progressive Web App (PWA) Functionality: Make your website installable directly onto a user's mobile home screen like a native app, which allows for faster loading and push notifications.
